Common questions

Pricing FAQs

More questions? See the full FAQ page →

Why is the website free? What's the catch? +
There's no catch. Instead of charging $3,000–$12,000 upfront like most agencies, we spread the cost into a monthly subscription. You get the same quality website — you just don't have to hand over a lump sum before seeing anything.
What happens if I want to cancel before 18 months? +
The 18-month minimum is a firm commitment — that's how we're able to offer a free build. If you need to exit early, we'll discuss your situation directly. We're a small operation, so we handle these things like adults, not like a big corporate with a rigid cancellation policy.
Can I switch plans after I sign up? +
Yes — you can upgrade at any time. Moving from PRESENCE to GROWTH or from GROWTH to AUTHORITY is easy and takes effect from your next billing cycle. Downgrading is possible at the end of a term.
Do I own my website? +
You own all the content on your website — your words, your photos, your brand. The Webflow build is tied to the subscription. If you end the subscription after your term, we'll discuss transfer options with you directly.
Is GST included in the prices? +
All prices shown are GST-exclusive. GST applies to Australian businesses where applicable. We'll show the full GST-inclusive figure on your invoice.
