Questions

Straight answers.

Everything Brisbane pool and outdoor-living builders typically ask before getting started. If your question isn't here, email me directly.

The free redesign
You really redesign my homepage for free?+

Yes. I rebuild your homepage — new design, new structure, new copy — and send it to you before you've committed to anything. No charge, no watermark, no obligation. The file is yours regardless of what you decide.

What exactly do I get in the free redesign?+

A fully designed homepage built to show your projects at their best, with a clear "request a quote" path, fast load, and copy that speaks to homeowners making a $60k–$150k decision. It's not a template — it's built specifically for your business, using your photos and your work.

How long does it take?+

Usually 48 hours from when you send me your current site and a handful of your best project photos. I'll let you know if it's going to take longer.

What if I hate it?+

Then we shake hands and you keep the file. No hard sell, no follow-up pressure. I only want to work with builders who can see the value in what I've built — if that's not you, there's no bad blood.

Pricing & contracts
How much does the full build cost?+

From $1,990 for a focused site — homepage, project gallery, about, contact. More pages or more complexity sits higher. I'll give you a fixed quote before you commit, so there are no surprises.

What's included in the monthly care plan?+

Hosting, speed and security updates, Google Business Profile management, review monitoring and responses, content updates (new project photos, copy tweaks), and a monthly performance check-in. From $390/mo.

Is there a lock-in contract?+

No. The build is a one-off. The care plan is month to month — cancel any time with no penalty. I keep your business by doing good work, not by making it painful to leave.

Do I have to take the care plan?+

No. The build is a standalone product. But most clients stay on the care plan because it handles everything they'd otherwise forget about — and it's the part that keeps leads coming in month after month.

Can I pay in instalments?+

Yes. I typically do 50% at sign-off and 50% at launch. If your cashflow works differently, let me know — I'm flexible.

The build & process
How long does the full site take to build?+

Two to three weeks from sign-off to live. Faster than most agencies because I focus on one niche — I'm not learning your industry from scratch each time.

I already have a website. Can you work with that?+

That's the most common situation I work in. The free redesign will show you exactly what's costing you quote requests right now, then rebuild around that. Most of the builders I work with already had a site — it just wasn't pulling its weight.

What platform do you build on?+

Webflow. It's fast, secure, and gives me design control without bloated page builders. Your site will load quickly, look great on any device, and be easy for me to update when you send through new project photos.

Can I update the site myself?+

Yes, Webflow has a simple editor for text and images. Most clients on the care plan prefer to just send me the new photos and copy, and I handle it — but either works.

Do you help with Google rankings?+

Yes, within the care plan. I set up Google Search Console, manage your Google Business Profile, monitor your local rankings for pool and outdoor-living searches in Brisbane, and make adjustments when needed. I don't promise page-one rankings in week one — I promise a site that's structured correctly and maintained properly so Google rewards it over time.

Working with me
Do you work with other kinds of businesses?+

No. I focus exclusively on pool builders, landscape builders, deck and pergola builders, and outdoor-living contractors. The narrow focus is the point — it's why the free redesign is fast, why the sites convert, and why I can price it the way I do.

Who actually builds the site?+

Me — Andres. ClawMark is just me, not an agency. You talk to the person who designs and builds your site from the first call to launch. No account managers, no offshore handoffs, no miscommunication.
