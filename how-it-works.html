The process

From dated site
to booked jobs.

No agency theatre, no 12-week timelines, no surprise invoices. Here's exactly what happens — and when.

Simple by design

Four steps. Two to three weeks. Done.

Most agencies make this complicated because complexity hides the value. I don't need to do that. The work speaks for itself — which is why you see it before you decide anything.

01

Free redesign — you see the work first

Send me your current site. I rebuild your homepage — new design, new copy, new structure — and send it back to you, usually within 48 hours. No charge. No obligation. No watermark. The file is yours to keep regardless of what you decide.

This is the whole offer. I can't think of a lower-risk way to see whether I'm the right fit for your business — so I made it the starting point for every project.

Takes 48 hours · Free · No obligation
02

We walk through it together

Once you've seen the redesign, we jump on a short call. I'll show you what I changed and why — what was costing you quote requests, and how the new version fixes it. If it's not clearly better than what you had, you walk away with the file. No hard feelings.

If you love it, we talk about the full build — scope, timeline, and the monthly care plan.

One short call · You decide after
03

I build and launch the full site

You approve the scope and I get to work. The full site — homepage, project gallery, about, contact, and any other pages you need — is built, tested, and live within two to three weeks. I handle domain setup, hosting, Google Search Console, and the technical bits so you don't have to.

You review at each stage. Nothing goes live without your sign-off.

2–3 weeks · You review at every stage
04

Your site goes to work — month after month

After launch, the care plan kicks in. Hosting, speed updates, Google Business Profile management, review responses, and the ongoing tweaks that keep your site ranking and converting. All included in the monthly. Cancel any time — no lock-in.

Most of my clients see a steady increase in quote requests in the first two to three months as Google catches up with the new site.

Month to month · No lock-in · Cancel any time
