The FG Dolls Experience

Where Every Appointment Feels Personal

From the moment you step into our West End studio, you're welcomed with a warm drink, a thorough consultation, and the undivided attention of a stylist who genuinely cares about your hair's long-term health and beauty.

  • Warm Welcome, Every Visit

    Coffee or tea on arrival — because great hair takes time, and we want you to enjoy every minute of it.

  • In-Depth Colour Consultations

    Every colour appointment begins with a thorough consultation. We listen first — your vision, your lifestyle, your hair history, all considered.

  • Hair Health First

    We never compromise your hair's integrity for a result. Colour and treatment choices always prioritise long-term health alongside beauty.

  • Rooted in West End

    Our studio is part of the West End community — progressive, creative, and proudly independent. Come exactly as you are.
