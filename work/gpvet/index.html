About GPVet

Brisbane's First GP‑Style Vet Clinic

GPVet is the first of its kind — built around the idea that accessing great vet care shouldn't be difficult. We're here to be your pet's long-term health partner, not just a one-off appointment.

Access When You Need It

When your pet is unwell, waiting days for an appointment is stressful. Same-day care is our standard — not a premium add-on.

Your Single Point of Contact

From primary care through to specialist referrals, we manage everything. No confusing hand-offs — just seamless, coordinated care for your pet.

Genuine Care, Every Time

Our team genuinely love animals. Every consultation focuses on both your pet's wellbeing and your peace of mind as their carer.

Our Team

The People Behind Your Pet's Care

Experienced, compassionate, and genuinely passionate about animals — meet the team at GPVet and GlamPet.

Dr Megan Pitcher

Head Veterinarian

BVSc (First Class Honours), University of Queensland. 25+ years in small animal practice across Australia and the UK. Associate Fellow of the Higher Education Association, teaching in the Veterinary and Medical faculties at UQ since 2006. Devoted mum to border collies Mollie and Albert.

Dr Brian Wong

Veterinarian · Dental Specialist

A passionate veterinarian specialising in dentistry and advanced oral care. Skilled in dental assessment, periodontal treatment, and oral surgery. Dr Brian combines precision and a gentle touch to deliver the best outcomes for every patient. Cat dad to Mui.

Amanda

Head Groomer · GlamPet

14 years of hands-on grooming experience across breed-specific trims, creative styling, and Asian fusion techniques. Known for her calm, gentle approach with even the most anxious pets. Amanda leads and trains the GlamPet team to a consistently exceptional standard.
