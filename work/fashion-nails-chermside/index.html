About Us

Craftsmanship You Can See. Care You Can Feel.

Fashion Nails Chermside has been a trusted name in Brisbane's beauty scene for over 8 years. Our founder Steven Tran and the dedicated team are passionate about making every client feel their best — whether it's a quick tidy-up or an intricate custom set.

  • 🎨
    Detail-Obsessed Nail Artists

    Steven specialises in complex, intricate nail designs. Bring your inspo — we'll make it happen and then some.

  • ✨
    Immaculate Salon Standards

    Clean, hygienic, and welcoming — our salon is maintained to the highest standards. Comfortable pedicure chairs with massage function.

  • 🤝
    Loyal Local Community

    Many of our clients have been coming to us for years — some for over 8. That loyalty is everything to us.
