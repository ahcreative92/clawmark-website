FAQ

Honest answers
to fair questions.

How much does a website cost? +

Your website is always free to build. You pay a monthly subscription starting from $129 for hosting, maintenance, and ongoing support. You never pay anything until you've seen and approved the design.

What's the catch with "free"? +

There isn't one — but we'll be upfront. The free design is how we show you we're serious before asking you to commit. The 18-month minimum subscription is how we recoup the build cost over time. It works out significantly cheaper than a traditional agency, and you get ongoing support included.

What happens after 18 months? +

After your minimum period, your subscription moves to a month-to-month arrangement. You can continue, upgrade, or cancel — the choice is yours. Your website and its content remain yours.

How long does it take to go live? +

We deliver your first working sketch within 72 hours of our initial call. After revisions and approval, your site typically goes live within 5–10 business days, depending on how quickly content and feedback come through.

Do you use templates? +

Never. Every Clawmark website is custom-designed from scratch in Webflow — built specifically for your brand, your audience, and your goals. You'll never see your layout on another website.

What if I don't like the design? +

We revise until you're happy — no extra charge. And if after revisions you still don't want to move forward, you can walk away at zero cost. We don't believe in charging for work you don't love.
